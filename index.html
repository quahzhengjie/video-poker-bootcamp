How to play

  1. You start with 100 points.

  2. After you click on "Deal" the computer will deal a hand of 5 cards. You may choose any number of their cards to replace with new, random cards.

  3. After replacing cards, the game assigns points based on the resulting hand. See rankings of Poker hands here.
